Return & Refund Policy

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, we've got you.

🔄 Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. Items must be in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached.

✅ Eligible for Returns

  • Items in original, unworn condition
  • Items with all original tags and packaging
  • Items purchased within the last 30 days

❌ Not Eligible for Returns

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Gift cards

📝 How to Start a Return

  1. Contact us at our contact page with your order number
  2. We'll send you a return shipping label via email
  3. Pack your item(s) securely in the original packaging
  4. Drop off the package at your nearest shipping location

💰 Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective product, etc.).

🔁 Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We're happy to help! Contact us to arrange an exchange. Exchanges are subject to availability.

📞 Questions?

If you have any questions, please contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
